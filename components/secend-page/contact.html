About Us

Description

"El-Mofaker" is an international refereed review published semi-annually (June-December) by the Faculty of Law and Political Science at the University of Mohamed Khider Biskra, Algeria. It specializes in the fields of law and political science, including public and private law in all its branches, political science in all its branches, as well as Sharia law, and public administration.

The review primarily aims to offer free publication—on paper and electronically—of original works, studies, and innovative research in three languages: Arabic, English, and French, for professors and researchers worldwide. It contributes to enriching scientific knowledge and developing specialized studies in the aforementioned fields.
